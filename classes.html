Beginners

In beginners classes we focus on the basics, starting from the ground up. Ideal if you are completely new to martial arts or nervous about getting started. No experience needed, all abilities welcome

Technique

The technique classes are structured to develop fundamental Striking Clinic techniques. Working in pairs to practice striking combinations that are demonstrated at the beginning of each class. All abilities welcome.

Precision MMA

Precision MMA classes are grappling based classes designed to teach you the skills you need to compete as an MMA athlete. All abilities welcome.

Bagwork

The Bag-Work classes are based around fitness and conditioning. The session is broken down into rounds where you can practice your technique, develop power and increase fitness. All abilities welcome.

Sparring

Sparring sessions are currently invite only. Once you can demonstrate a level of competency during technique classes you will be invited to join in on regular sparring sessions but of course sparring is optional.

Little Vipers

We created our Little Vipers program to give your children the best start to their martial arts journey. We focus on coaching techniques that are not only applicable to MMA but highly effective in self defence too.
